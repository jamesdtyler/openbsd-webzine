Shell tips

Did you know your shell had keyboard shortcuts? They are absolutely useful, here is a list for the default mode (called emacs mode):

  • ctrl+r: search in history
  • ctrl+a and ctrl+e : move cursor to beginning or end of the line
  • alt+d and alt+backspace : delete the word after or before the cursor
  • ctrl+k and ctrl+u : delete the text after the cursor or before the cursor
  • ctrl+y: paste the last deleted text
The shell can also be used in vi mode but this I keep it for another webzine issue.
